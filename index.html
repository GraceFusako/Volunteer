Future IN Design

Technology in the Margins

Future IN Design (FIND) is a STEAM career development program for marginalized young adults. Our mission is to narrow the digital literacy gap in Utah through immersive training in key technology and life skills, thereby reducing constrains of intergenerational poverty, and creating a pipeline of talent for Utah's growing tech workforce.
